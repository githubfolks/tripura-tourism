About Us
Tripura Nature Trails and Resorts Ltd. (TNTR) was established on 21st November, 2022, as a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) under the purview of the Tripura Forest Department (TFD). The primary objective of TNTR is to spearhead the development and promotion of Eco-Tourism within the state of Tripura, thereby fostering a harmonious synergy between tourism, growth and environmental preservation.
Covering a sprawling area of 6,292.68 square kilometres, Tripura boasts extensive forest regions, constituting nearly 59.98% of the state's total landmass. Moreover, approximately 60% of its terrain is characterized by rugged hills. Nestled amidst the adventure havens of Northeast India, Tripura emerges as an irresistible magnet for travellers, enticing them with its myriad natural wonders and cultural splendours. The grandeur of Himalayan foothills draped in dense forest cover, whispering bamboo groves, vast waterscapes mirroring the skies, enchanting nature trails, offers sanctuary not only to the wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers but also to a range of adventure seekers. Moreover, amidst these natural wonders, one may find a diverse mosaic of indigenous cultures, each with its own stories of resilience, tradition, and heritage woven into its fabric.
Recognizing the immense potential inherent in this unexplored realm, TNTR Ltd. emerges as a pivotal entity, envisioning itself as the catalyst for transformative initiatives. Its mission encompasses the crafting of immersive experiences that not only showcase the region's ecological treasures but also foster community empowerment. Through a commitment to eco-tourism development, TNTR Ltd. aspires to contribute meaningfully to the sustainable growth of the state while preserving its natural heritage.
