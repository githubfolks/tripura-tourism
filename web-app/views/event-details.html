About the Event

The Neermahal Water Festival is one of the most vibrant celebrations in Tripura, held annually at the iconic Rudrasagar Lake. Centered around the majestic Neermahal ("Water Palace"), built by King Bir Bikram Kishore Manikya in 1930, this festival brings the lake to life with energy, color, and tradition.

The highlight of the festival is the thrilling boat race, where traditional long boats competed against each other to the rhythm of drums and cheers from thousands of spectators lining the shores. Beyond the races, visitors can witness mesmerizing swimming competitions and indulge in local cultural programs featuring folk music and dance that continue late into the evening.

The palace itself is illuminated magnificently, casting a golden reflection on the waters of Rudrasagar, creating a magical atmosphere for photographers and tourists alike.
